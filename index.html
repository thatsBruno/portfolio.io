About Me

Hi, I'm Bruno, a Software Engineer who is open-minded, inquisitive, and a life-long learner, with a mentorship mentality and a passion for helping other developers grow professionally.

A bit about my background: I graduated from DeMontfort University, UK, and after returning home, I started working at Nokia, where I gained experience in network integration. A few months later, I joined Siemens, where I worked on a mature project and had the opportunity to learn extensively about microservices, CI/CD, the .NET ecosystem, Angular, and the full flow of web apps from backend to frontend, up to the deployment of databases and services on AWS.

Currently, I'm working on an Industrial Edge project at Siemens, where I contribute to code reviews, architectural decisions and thrive in a dynamic enterprise environment, as well as developing features and plugins in Go for both virtual and physical devices, and handle deployment and testing on GitLab’s pipeline.

When I'm not working, you'll find me tinkering with new frameworks or fine-tuning my VSCode config with Vim keybinds.

Outside of tech, I enjoy doing CrossFit and reading bite-sized books on apps, mostly about technology or futuristic concepts involving tech.
